# Welcome to Scopien AI Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for Scopien AI, your intelligent Salesforce assistant platform. This documentation will help you get started, understand features, and make the most of our AI-powered automation.

## What is Scopien AI?

Scopien AI transforms how you interact with Salesforce by replacing hours of manual clicking with simple conversations. Our AI-powered platform understands your needs and automates complex Salesforce tasks instantly.
